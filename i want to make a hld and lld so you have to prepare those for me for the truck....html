High-Level Design (HLD)

1. System Overview

The Truck Fleet Management system is designed to enable owners and drivers to efficiently manage long-distance trucking operations with a focus on safety and compliance. The system allows owners to manage trucks, invite drivers, create trips, and track detailed trip data such as driving sessions, rest logs, and fuel usage. Drivers report their trip progress manually, including start/end of drive sessions, rest periods, and refueling events. Real-time alerts notify owners and drivers when driving safety limits are exceeded to prevent accidents.

2. System Architecture

Layered Architecture:

LayerDescription
Presentation LayerReact frontend provides user interaction and dashboard views for owners and drivers.
Application LayerNode.js with Express RESTful APIs serves business logic and handles client requests.
Domain LayerImplements core business rules such as rest compliance, trip progress estimation, and notification triggers.
Data LayerMongoDB stores all entities—users, trucks, trips, drive and rest sessions, refuel logs, notifications.

Key Components:

  • User Authentication & Role Management (Owner, Driver)

  • Truck and Driver Management

  • Trip Management (Create, Assign, Update, Complete)

  • Drive Session and Rest Period Logging

  • Fuel Refuel Event Logging

  • Compliance Alerts and Real-Time Notifications

  • Dashboard and Reporting

3. Modules and Their Responsibilities

ModulePurposeKey Features

User ManagementInvite and register drivers, user profile managementInvite tokens, driver registration via invite

DriveSession ModuleTrack individual driving sessionsStart/end timestamps, fuel usage, km covered, compliance checks with alerting
RestLog ModuleRecord rest periods between driving sessionsStart/end times, fuel status during rest
RefuelEvent ModuleLog and track refueling events during tripsFuel before/after, payment mode, event timestamps
Notification ModuleAlert owners and drivers about compliance and system eventsNotification CRUD, seen/unseen management
Dashboard ModuleAggregate and report key stats to usersTruck counts, trip stats, ongoing trips, recent activities

4. User & Data Flow

  1. Owner Actions

    • Create trucks.

    • Invite drivers via email with tokenized invites.

    • Create trips with truck, driver, trip details.

    • View dashboard with analytics and compliance data.

  2. Driver Actions

    • Register using invite token.

    • Log in and view assigned trips.

    • Manually start/end drive sessions, rest periods.

    • Log refuel events.

    • Receive notifications for compliance warnings and trip status.

  3. During Trips

    • Drive sessions and rest logs are entered by drivers.

    • Continuous driving is monitored; alerts triggered after 4 hours.

    • Daily cumulative driving is monitored; alerts triggered after 8 hours.

    • Fuel consumption and distance are estimated from refuel and mileage data.

    • Trip updates and completion status are communicated to owner and driver.
